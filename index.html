Frequently Asked Questions

MyCirkleDevelopment (commonly referred to as MyCirkle) is an innovative loyalty account program designed to reward both new and returning customers for their continued support and engagement. By participating in simple, interactive activities such as completing tasks, joining events, and taking part in promotions, users can earn points and access exclusive rewards tailored to their preferred platforms.

The primary goal of MyCirkle is to help customers save money and gain extra benefits while engaging with the brand's community-based services. Whether you're a casual visitor or a dedicated supporter, MyCirkle offers opportunities to receive digital rewards, discounts, and unique perks for your involvement.

As a loyalty program still in heavy development and BETA testing, MyCirkle will continue to evolve with user feedback, adding new features, rewards, and integrations over time. MyCirkle is not only a loyalty program, but it is a new family for those returning and new customers.

You can earn points by:

  • Making purchases from Cirkle Development
  • Completing tasks and challenges
  • Participating in events and promotions
  • Engaging with the community
  • Claiming your daily rewards

To redeem your rewards, please open a ticket in our Discord Server. Our support team will assist you with:

  • Processing reward redemptions
  • Verifying your scratch card codes
  • Answering questions about available rewards
  • Helping with any redemption issues

For general support, enquiries, and redemptions, please open a ticket in the Discord Server.

For account loss, details, TOS/&C, and Privacy Policy matters, please email: mycirkle@cirkledevelopment.co.uk

Your points can be redeemed for:

  • Discount codes for future purchases
  • Free shipping vouchers
  • Exclusive digital rewards
  • Special perks and benefits
  • Early access to new products and events

If you've lost your account details, please email us at: mycirkle@cirkledevelopment.co.uk with your Discord username and any information you can provide about your account.
